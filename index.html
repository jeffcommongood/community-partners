211 Sacramento is a social, health and disaster services information hub maintaining a network of over 1600 community resources for the Sacramento County area. 2-1-1 is accessible 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to help community members navigate the social and health delivery systems in times of need with services in English, Spanish, Lao, Mien, and Thai in addition to over 200 languages and dialects via tele-interpreter service.
